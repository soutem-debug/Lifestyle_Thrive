The Introduction

They say it takes a village, and mums went without their village in one of their most testing times in their life. Everybody forgets that when a baby is born, a mother is born too, and she too needs that same love, nourishment and rich environment in order to thrive.

So after a year of the outside world being on pause, where you could actually get to really focus on every little detail (the good and the bad!) of your baby (and you!), you most certainly have learnt the things that you value most. These are the things to carry through as the world opens up again. The vibes that you want entering your precious circle, the places you go which have value, and the things that you do which have meaning.
