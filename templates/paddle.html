The Introduction

Paddle boarding is fast becoming one of the most popular water sports available to do. It is not only a great low impact work out it is a brilliant stress reliever due to the calming nature of the water around and of course falling off the board can give you a serious adrenaline rush to distract from any negative energy you are holding on to. With just your SUP in hand, it is difficult to think about any of your past worries.

  • Gets you on your feet
  • Helps develop balance and coordination
  • Reduces stress levels – yes really
  • Low impact workout
  • Can help with weight loss
  • Great for cardio
  • It provides a tranquil environment to decompress in

Paddle boarding during lockdown?

Unless you are lucky enough to live by the sea the chances are getting out on your SUP board has not been easy during pandemic. But worry not there are plenty of places to get out to satisfy your SUP needs before travel outside your area is allowed again. The Leeds and Liverpool canal now forms part of the English coast to coast route, where you can paddle from the west to the east coast of England if you so wish to, just remember it’s easier to paddle East then against the current West!
